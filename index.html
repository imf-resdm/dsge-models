DSGE Cloud Modeling App

Use this application to run deterministic simulations of some of the DSGE models developed in the Research Department at the International Monetary Fund. You can calibrate each model to a certain country (or group of countries) and use country-specific paths for exogenous variables.

Current Account Norm Model

Based on Current Account Norms in Natural Resource Rich and Capital Scarce Economies by Araujo et al. (2013).

Debt Investment Growth (DIG) Model

Based on Public Investment, Growth, and Debt Sustainability: Putting Together the Pieces by Buffie et al. (2012).

Run this model »

Debt Investment Growth & Natural Resource (DIGNAR) Model

Based on Debt Sustainability, Public Investment, and Natural Resources in Developing Countries: the DIGNAR Model by Melina et al. (2014).

This application was developed as part of a project on macroeconomic policy in low-income countries supported by the UK's Department for International Development.
